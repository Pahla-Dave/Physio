Our Specialized Services

Cardiovascular Physiotherapy

This is the prevention, rehabilitation and comansation of individuals with diseases and injuries in the heart and lungs. These conditions may manifest as shortness of breath, persistent coughing, increased breathing rate or a decrese in breathing rate.

Neuromuscular Physiotherapy

This a specialised area in Physiotherapy focusing on treating individuals with neurological conditions. These affect the functions of the brain, spinal cord and nerves.Stroke, Mulitiple Sclerosis, Spinal Cord Injury and Parkinson's being the common nuerological conditions. We as specialist are trained to handle such cases with the main aim being to aid the individual in regaining maximum movement and functional independence.

Respiratory Physiotherapy

This is the the improvment of ones breathing by inirect removal of mucus from ones breathing passages. This is perfomed on people suffering from mucus dysfunction in respiratory disease conditions like asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, bronchtis, bronchiectasis and cystic fibrosis.

Physiotherapy in Neuromusculoskeletal

This is the treatment of bones and muscles (especially spinal pain in the lower back)by employing advanced clinical assesment and diagnosis methods. We are here to aid those who may expirience lower back, neck pain and joint problems, we even eduate our patients to prevent such future problems.

Acupuncture

We provide targeted therapy for pain management. In chronic pain conditions like low back pain, osteoarthritis and neck pain, it the release of endorphins and other neurochemicals that reduce pain perception.
